Welcome to Slides Captions

Slides Captions forces your Google Slides captions into multiple lines. It only works when the font size is set to Large in Google Slides Caption Settings.

To use the extension you must

  • Ensure that your captions are set to appear at the top and in large text
  • Load your presentation in presentation view, exit full screen and then reload the presentation - alternatively change the end of your URL to /present
  • Toggle your captions on, once the screen is black

The extension will stop your slides from presenting. Disable the extension by clicking below

You can re-enable the extension from chrome://extensions
